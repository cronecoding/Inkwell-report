Rebecca Kimble, MD, MPH

Research and AI Engineering Lead

Rebecca is a multidisciplinary researcher with a background in medicine, public health, and data science. She specializes in translating complex global issues into evidence-based insights and visual narratives. Her work supports actionable decision-making in crisis environments, with a focus on equity, accuracy, and ethical AI.

She has previously served with humanitarian organizations and government partnerships to develop tools for real-time response and long-term forecasting. At Inkwell, she leads the integration of advanced data systems into our publication and analysis workflow.
