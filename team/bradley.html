Ann Bradley, BSc International Relations

International Relations and Cultural Insight Analyst

Ann is an international relations analyst with a degree from American University and a deep understanding of the intersection between politics, culture, and everyday life in Washington, D.C. Her academic training, combined with years of direct experience navigating political dynamics in high-profile settings, gives her a grounded, people-first perspective on diplomacy, discourse, and soft power. She brings curiosity, insight, and lived fluency in the complexity of U.S. political culture. Ann speaks fluent French and has spent significant time abroad.
