Who We Are

Inkwell brings together a diverse team of experts in public health, environmental sustainability, international relations, harm reduction, data science, nonprofit strategy, and health equity. From Vermont to South Africa, Pittsburgh to India, each voice adds depth, insight, and lived experience to the stories we tell and the systems we aim to shift.
