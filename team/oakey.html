Karen Oakey, MSc HR

People, Strategy, and Organizational Consultant

Karen Oakey is a seasoned organizational leader with extensive experience guiding people strategies, leadership development, and human resources transformation across a wide range of industries. She has a proven track record of designing innovative HR frameworks that align talent, culture, and operational goals to drive business success.

Throughout her career, Karen has partnered with senior leadership teams to build high-performing cultures, support change management initiatives, and create sustainable structures that foster employee engagement and growth. Her approach is grounded in data-driven insights, cross-functional collaboration, and an unwavering commitment to equity and inclusion.

At Inkwell Global Report, Karen brings expertise in organizational development, strategic consulting, and leadership coaching. Her ability to connect people-centered solutions with broader operational needs makes her a vital contributor to multidisciplinary projects that require systems thinking and human-centered design.

Karen holds a Master of Science in Human Resources (MSc HR) and has worked across the United States, leading initiatives that prepare organizations to thrive in rapidly evolving environments.
