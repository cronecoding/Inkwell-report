Lauren Boczkowski, MPH

Health Policy Consultant

Lauren Boczkowski is a public health researcher and strategist specializing in health systems, communications, and policy initiatives. She brings a deep understanding of how government partnerships, nonprofits, and communities intersect to shape health outcomes and advance equity.

Over the course of her career, Lauren has led initiatives across a range of organizations—from city governments to international NGOs—building coalitions, conducting program evaluations, and using data-driven insights to inform sustainable change. Her approach is rooted in a commitment to accessible, transparent, and actionable public health strategies.

At Inkwell Global Report, Lauren contributes expertise in systems thinking, data storytelling, and collaborative project design. She is passionate about translating research into meaningful action and helping organizations meet today’s complex public health challenges.

Lauren holds a Master of Public Health (MPH) from the Brown University School of Public Health and a BA from the University of Pennsylvania. She is a frequent collaborator on public health initiatives spanning local, national, and international contexts.
